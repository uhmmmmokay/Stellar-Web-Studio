Frequently Asked Questions

Do dental treatments hurt?
Modern dentistry uses painless techniques and anesthesia to ensure your comfort throughout every procedure.
How often should I visit the dentist?
Most patients should visit every 6 months for preventive care and early detection of any issues.
Do you accept insurance?
Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. See our Insurance page for details on accepted providers.
What should I bring to my first appointment?
Insurance card, ID, and completed patient forms. We provide forms online for your convenience.
Do you offer sedation dentistry?
Yes, we provide sedation options for anxious patients to make visits more relaxing.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and financing options like CareCredit. Insurance is billed directly.
Are you open on weekends?
We offer limited Saturday hours from 9AM to 2PM for your convenience.
How long does a teeth whitening session take?
Our in-office whitening typically takes about 1 hour, with immediate results.
